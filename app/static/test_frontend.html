Audio Intelligence Model

This system conducts automated audio signal processing and language parsing to analyze verbal communication quality immediately.

  • Fluency & Pace scoring: Calculates words-per-minute and checks against conversational standards.
  • Vocal tone features: Measures pitch variation, signal energy level, volume stability, and environmental noise scores.
  • Linguistic sentiment: Assesses speech semantics to score positive, neutral, or negative sentiment.
  • Filler words tracker: Automatically detects and highlights conversational filler tokens.
  • Behavioral insight system: Summarizes structured strengths, faults, and advice.
Ensure your microphone permissions are granted for capturing high quality speech. Works best with normal conversational speech lengths.
